- ### Refreshers
	- #### Spaces of Numbers
	  collapsed:: true
	  
	  $$\N \subset \mathbbm{Z} \subset Q \subset \R \subset C$$
		- #### Notable Operations
		  * $+$ and $*$ work fine in all spaces.
		  * $-$ does not always work in $\N$
		  * $/$ does not work when dividing by zero
		- #### Notable Facts
		  A **field** (Q, $\R$, and $C$) guarantee a conjugate of every set element.
