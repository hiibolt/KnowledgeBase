## Key Terms
	- ### Speed
	  $$\frac{\text{distance}}{\text{time}}$$
	  $$\frac{d_1-d_0}{t_1-t_0}$$
	  **__Not__ a vector**
	- ### Velocity
	  $$\frac{\text{directional distance}}{\text{time}}$$
	  $$\frac{d_1-d_0}{t_1-t_0}$$
	  **__Is__ a vector**
	- #### Scalar Quantity
	  Is a vector without direction.
