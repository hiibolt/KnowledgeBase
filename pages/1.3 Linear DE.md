## Base Equation:
\begin{equation*}
\{
\begin{array}{}
    a_i = ra_{a-1}       i = 1,2,3 \\
    a_0
\end{array}
\end{equation*}
- ### Edge Cases
	- #### -1 < r < 1
	  $a_n = 0$
	- #### R = 1
	  $a_n = a_0$
	- #### r < -1, |r| > 1
	  $a_i > 0$ if i is even
	  $a_i < 0$ if i is odd
	- #### r = -1
	  $a_i = +_ a_0$
