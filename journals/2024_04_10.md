	  \begin{cases}
	  \frac{dx}{dt}=x\\
	  \frac{dy}{dt}=y
	  \end{cases}
	  
	  $$\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{y}{x}$$
	  $$\text{so}$$
	  $$\ln|y|=\ln|x|+C$$
	  $$\text{or}$$
	  $$y=Cx$$
